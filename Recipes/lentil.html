Lentil Soup

Lentil soup is commenly consumed in Europe,Latin America and Middle Eastern.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • A dry onion
  • A tablespoon of flour
  • Half a tablespoon of tomato paste
  • A Carrot
  • A cup of red lentils
  • A litre of Water
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Cumin
  • 2 tablespoon of butter
  • A teaspoon of red powdered pepper

    • Recipe

    1. First, pour 2 tablespoons of oil into the pot,
    2. Then chop a dry onion and add into the pot and bake it until it forms into pinkish color.
    3. Add a tablespoon of flour into the pot and continue to mix.
    4. Add half a tablespoon of tomato paste.
    5. Dice the carrot into cube pieces and then add it into the pot.
    6. Add a cup off red lentils into the pot.
    7. A a litre of water into the pot.
    8. Cook the mixture until carrot pieces and red lentils get soft.
    9. The cooked soup is passed through the blender.
    10. Add black pepper and salt.
    11. The water can be added if there is an desire to change consistency of soup.
    12. Add 2 tablespoon of butter and a teaspoon of red powdered pepper.
